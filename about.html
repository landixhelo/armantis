About Us

EXPLORE

Let Us Care Of Your Experience

Armantis provides air-conditioned rooms in Sarandë. This beachfront property offers access to free Wifi and free private parking. When staying at the apartment, guests can use private entrance.
Armantis provides guests with a balcony, a seating area, satellite flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and a fridge, and a private bathroom with walk-in shower and a hair dryer.
A stovetop and kitchenware are also provided, as well as a kettle. At the apartment complex, all units are equipped with bed linen and towels.
Guests at the accommodation can enjoy fishing nearby, or make the most of the sun terrace. Popular points of interest near Armantis include Mango Beach, Heaven Beach, and Flamingo Beach. The nearest airport is Corfu International Airport, 25 miles from the apartment.
